Reflection

I have really enjoyed taking part in this project. In my past i have always enjoyed using code such as python and delphi however never touched on HTML or CSS. This meant that the coding tasks came quite naturally to me and was the first task i completed. I found it very interesting how basic websites can be made using html and seeing the extent that they can be styled using CSS.

The design side of the course was all completely new to me, however still really enjoyable when learning how to use InDesign and Illustrator. I picked up Illustrator really quickly and am very happy with my typography designs. I feel that my CARP posters are my weakpoint as i was jumping into it with no background knowledge except what i was taught in lectures. This made it difficult for me to picture designs and apply them to InDesign. I would have liked to experiment with InDesign a bit more as i did with Illustrator.

I planned my website to be very straight forward and minimilistic to ensure its easy to use for any type of user. I have done this by creating multiple pages with easy to use links and layouts. Users of my website may include my lecturer (Mary) and other staff for marking and feedback, possible employers for future work as well as friends and family who are interested in my work.

Although my website isn't perfect, it's the perfect basis for a more complex website that i could be making in the future. Some tasks may not be to the standard that i wanted but i am still very pleased with how my website has turned out.
